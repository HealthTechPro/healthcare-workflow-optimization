# AI Workflow Opportunities

This file outlines future-state opportunities for applying AI and automation to healthcare workflow operations.

---

## AI-Assisted Payer Communication

AI tools could support:

- Retrieving payer contact information
- Automating repetitive payer calls
- Reducing administrative workload
- Improving documentation verification processes

---

## Documentation Workflow Optimization

AI-assisted tools could:

- Identify missing discharge summaries
- Flag incomplete documentation
- Predict delays based on case patterns
- Suggest next actions for case resolution

---

## Peer-to-Peer Review Optimization

Peer-to-peer reviews require significant provider time.

AI could assist by:

- Preparing documentation summaries
- Organizing clinical notes
- Reducing provider administrative burden
- Improving turnaround time for approvals

---

## Operational Efficiency Opportunities

- Automating workflow tracking
- Reducing duplicate work across reviewers
- Improving case prioritization using data patterns

---

## Future Vision

The integration of AI into healthcare operations can:

- Improve efficiency
- Reduce delays
- Support clinical and administrative teams
- Enhance overall system performance
